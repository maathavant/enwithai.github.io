# Our Platform Vision: EnginOS

EnginOS is the foundational Operating System for Engineering Intelligence.

**EnginOS is:**

- A unified engineering brain that connects code, systems, people, and processes
- A semantic model that understands *intent*, not just tasks
- A platform that allows rapid creation of AI agents and engineering assistants
- A flexible, scalable layer that works with existing tools

**EnginOS unlocks:**

- Intelligent workflows
- Cross-system reasoning
- Knowledge continuity
- Fast rollout of AI-native products like CrewPE
