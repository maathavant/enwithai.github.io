# About EnWithAI

**Who we are**

EnWithAI builds intelligence-first systems that make engineering work feel natural, coherent, and faster.

**Why the name matters**

“Withai” in Tamil means *seed*.

EnWithAI represents the seed of intelligence planted inside engineering workflows — designed to grow into platforms, products, and agents that simplify life for engineering teams.

**Mission**

To turn every engineering organization into an intelligence-native organization without demanding behavioral change.
