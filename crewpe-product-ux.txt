Introducing CrewPE - Experience on top of Platform

CrewPE is our first product built on EnginOS.

An AI-first Engineering Lifecycle Platform that acts like the smart partner your engineering team has always needed.

**CrewPE delivers:**

- Auto-generated requirements, stories, and test flows
- Code insights, PR reviews, and architecture guidance
- Auto-summarized sprints, releases, and incidents
- Early detection of bottlenecks, risks, and dependencies
- A knowledge graph that remembers decisions, reasoning, and patterns

Designed to embed intelligence, not add another tool.
